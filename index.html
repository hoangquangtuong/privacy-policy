Privacy - Privacy Policy

This privacy policy has been compiled to better serve those who are concerned about how their 'Personally Identifiable Information' (PII) is being used online. PII, as described in US privacy and information security laws, is information that can be used alone or in combination with other information to identify, contact, or locate a person, or to identify form of an individual in context. Please read our privacy policy carefully to understand how we collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your Personally Identifiable Information under our Extensions.

What personal information do we collect from extension users?

We do NOT collect any Personally Identifiable Information. We only collect extension usage statistics to help improve user experience. If you want to opt out of Google Analytics tracking, please visit https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

When do we collect information?

We only collect PII (your name) when you message us directly for customer support to allow us to better serve you in responding to your customer service requests and contact you after the exchange (Facebook Chat).

How do we use extension usage statistics?

We use extension usage statistics in the following way:

  • To see which features are used by most users.
  • To improve our extensions to serve you better.

How do we protect your information?

We do not use PCI scanning and/or vulnerability scanning. We only provide exclusive features to improve your Facebook experience. We never ask for credit card numbers. Again we do not store or collect any user information.

Your personal information is stored behind your Browser's LocalStorage and is accessible only to you. Additionally, when you log out of Facebook, LocalStorage is deleted so no one else can access it.

Do we use 'cookies'?

We use cookies to make the entire extension work.

Instead of asking users to log in or out, we use cookies. Additionally, most features from J2TEAM Security use Facebook APIs that require cookies. However, no cookies are collected!

California Online Privacy Protection Act

CalOPPA is the first state law in the nation to require online commerce and service websites to post privacy policies. The scope of this law goes beyond California to require any person or company in the United States (and possibly the world) that operates websites collecting Personally Identifiable Information from California consumers to post clear privacy policy on its website stating exactly what information is collected and what it does. the individuals or companies with whom it is being shared. - See more at: http://consumercal.org/california-online-privacy-protection-act-caloppa/#sthash.0FdRbT51.dpuf

Under CalOPPA, we agree to the following:

  • Users can visit our website anonymously.
  • Once this privacy policy is created, we will add a link to it on our home page or at least on the first significant page after entering our website.
